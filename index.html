Vinod Chavan

Profile

In a world where creativity reigns supreme, Vinod, a visionary co-founder of Grofame Productions, was born with a spark that would ignite a firestorm of innovation!

Vinod's artistic journey began at Bhavan's College, where he perfected his acting and dancing skills. He then ominated theatre competitions and landed a coveted role in 'Devmanus', making his mark on the global stage!

But fate had more in store for Vinod. His passion for animation led him to the esteemed Arena Institute, where he mastered the craft. The world of animation opened its doors, and Vinod seized the opportunity, working on iconic projects like 'Freeze 2', '3-2-1-Penguins', and 'Alpha and Omega'.

As a Team Leader and Supervisor at Crest Animation Studios, Vinod's creative genius shone bright on Hollywood blockbusters like 'How to Train Your Dragons, Transformers', and 'Casper. Bollywood's megahit 'Ra-One' also bore his signature touch.

With a wealth of experience and a burning desire to create something new, Vinod joined forces with Bhushan, and GROFAME PRODUCTIONS was born! A dream took flight, and the entertainment world would never be the same!

Bhushan, a visionary entrepreneur, dared to chase his dreams in the captivating world of entertainment! With an unrelenting passion ignited in his teenage years, he embarked on a journey to make his mark.

From the realms of advertising to the vibrant canvas of animation, Bhushan's odyssey was marked by an insatiable thirst for excellence. He honed his skills at Zest Advertising, MAAC, and Micro-solutions, before joining the esteemed Crest Animation Studios.

As a creative force, Bhushan breathed life into iconic projects like MBC, WordWorld, Barnyard, and Ra-One, leaving an indelible mark on the industry. His collaboration with Hollywood banners and Bollywood elite was a testament to his innovative spirit.
